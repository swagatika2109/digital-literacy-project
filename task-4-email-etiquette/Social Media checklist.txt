Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

Do’s:
1. Maintain a professional and respectful profile.
2. Think before posting any content online.
3. Use privacy settings to control who can see your information.
4. Share useful and positive content related to academics or skills.
5. Be respectful and polite while interacting with others online.

Don’ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
2. Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content.
3. Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying.
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown messages.
5. Do not use social media excessively during study time.
